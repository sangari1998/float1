ABOUT HER

  • Who she is
    • Outline
    • Born in 1994, she is a Singaporean American female pianist.
      She started playing the piano after her sister when she was young and has been playing since she was 4.
  • Who she did
    • Award History
    • In 2008, won 2th place in the IIYM competition.
      finished 3rd in the 2010 Thomas Evon Cooper International Competition, also won 1th place in the New York International Competition.
      Won 3rd place in the 2011 Rochester Eastman Young Artists Competition.

Activities After Contest

She has been out of action for a few years because of a shoulder injury. Then she visited Korea twice in 2019 and 2022 to perform Chopin Conert.

In November 2019, she performed with Eric Lu, who won fourth place in the 2015 competition together, and Chang-yong Shin, who won first place in the Gina Bakauer International Artists Competition in 2018 at Lotte Conert Hall

In September 2022, she performed Concert at the Seoul Arts Center with Dong-min Lim, who tied for third place in the Chopin Competition in 2005, and Leonora Armelini, who won fifth place in the Chopin Competition in 2021.
